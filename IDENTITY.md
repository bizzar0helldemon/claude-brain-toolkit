# Identity Profile

> This is your personal profile — the document that helps Claude understand who you are as a person, not just your code. Fill it in through `/brain-intake` sessions or by editing directly.

---

## Life Story

> [NEEDS INTAKE] Your background, where you come from, formative experiences, and what shaped you into who you are today. This isn't a resume — it's the human context that helps Claude understand your perspective.

---

## Career History

> [NEEDS INTAKE] Your professional journey — roles, transitions, what you've built, and what you've learned. Include both the official timeline and the real story behind the moves. What drove your career decisions? What are you most proud of professionally?

---

## Creative Voice

> [NEEDS INTAKE] Your creative identity — what you make, why you make it, and what your aesthetic sensibility looks like. This covers any creative domain: music, writing, visual art, video, game design, comedy, performance, etc. What themes keep showing up in your work? What influences shaped your taste?

---

## Values & Worldview

> [NEEDS INTAKE] What you believe, what you stand for, and how you see the world. This helps Claude understand the lens through which you evaluate ideas, make decisions, and interact with others. Not a manifesto — just the operating principles that matter to you.

---

## Communication Preferences

> [NEEDS INTAKE] How you like to communicate and be communicated with. Covers things like:
>
> - **Tone:** Do you prefer direct/blunt, warm/supportive, irreverent/funny, formal/professional?
> - **Length:** Do you want concise bullets or detailed explanations?
> - **Challenge level:** Should Claude push back on your ideas or just execute?
> - **Humor:** What kind of humor do you appreciate? What falls flat?
> - **Pet peeves:** Anything that annoys you in how AI typically responds?
